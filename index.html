Sensors Library

Servo Motor

A motor that moves to a specific position using control signals. It uses a feedback mechanism to achieve accurate positioning and is commonly employed in robotics, CNC machinery, camera systems, and automation applications.

Ultrasonic Sensor

Measures distance by sending ultrasonic waves and detecting echoes. Commonly used in robotics, automation, and obstacle detection systems.

Distance_IR

An infrared distance sensor uses infrared light to measure the proximity of objects. It emits an infrared beam and detects the reflected light to calculate distance.

QtPy

The QtPy is a compact and versatile microcontroller board designed for quick prototyping and small-scale projects. Ideal for wearable devices, IoT applications, and compact embedded systems.

ItsyBitsy ATmega32u4

The ItsyBitsy ATmega32u4 is a miniature microcontroller board. Perfect for space-constrained projects like wearable devices, custom keyboards, or portable electronics.

Linear Potentiometer (slider)

A linear potentiometer is a variable resistor that adjusts resistance linearly based on the slider's position. It is commonly used to control voltage or signal levels, such as adjusting volume or brightness.

Linear Potentiometer (soft)

A linear potentiometer is a variable resistor that adjusts resistance linearly based on the slider's position. It is commonly used to control voltage or signal levels, such as adjusting volume or brightness.

Long Flex Sensor

A long flex sensor is a variable resistor that changes resistance based on bending or flexing. It is commonly used to detect movement, angles, or bending gestures in applications like wearable devices, robotics, and motion-controlled interfaces.

Raspberry Pi

A Raspberry Pi is a small, affordable single-board computer designed for educational and hobbyist purposes. It is equipped with USB ports, HDMI output, and GPIO pins, making it ideal for programming, electronics projects, IoT applications, and as a media center.

MicroSD Memory Card

A MicroSD Memory Card is a compact storage device used to store data, including operating systems, applications, and files. It is essential for devices like Raspberry Pi as it serves as the primary storage for the operating system and user data.

Toggle Switch

A toggle switch is a mechanical switch that opens or closes an electrical circuit by moving a lever back and forth. It is commonly used to power devices on and off, control light fixtures, or switch between different modes in electronic projects.

Pulse Sensor

A pulse sensor is a heart rate monitoring device that measures the pulse rate by detecting blood flow through the fingertips or earlobes. It is widely used in fitness tracking, health monitoring, and biofeedback applications.

8X8 Neopixel LED Array

An 8x8 Neopixel LED array is a grid of individually addressable RGB LEDs, capable of displaying complex light patterns and animations. It is used for creating vibrant displays, dynamic indicators, and decorative lighting effects in interactive projects.

Breadboard Trim Potentiometer

A breadboard trim potentiometer is a small, adjustable resistor used to fine-tune voltage or current in a circuit. It is commonly used for calibrating sensors, adjusting signal levels, or setting reference voltages in prototyping and development.

LCD Display

An LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) is a flat-panel display that uses liquid crystals to produce images. It is commonly used to display text, numbers, and basic graphics in embedded systems, digital meters, and interactive projects.

Piezoelectric Sensor

A piezoelectric sensor converts mechanical pressure or vibration into electrical signals. It is used in applications like vibration detection, sound pickups, impact sensing, and touch-sensitive input devices.

PiOLED

The PiOLED is a small OLED display designed for Raspberry Pi, providing high-contrast visuals for text and basic graphics. It is used for displaying system status, sensor data, or notifications in compact projects.

Snap Switch_Limit Switch

A snap switch, also known as a limit switch, is a mechanical switch that activates when its lever is pressed. It is commonly used in industrial machines, 3D printers, and robotics for position detection and safety interlocks.

Speakers

Speakers convert electrical signals into sound, allowing audio output in devices. They are commonly used in multimedia projects, alarms, and interactive systems to provide audio feedback or play music.

Tactile Button Switch

A tactile button switch is a small, momentary push-button that makes an electrical connection when pressed. It is used for user inputs in electronics, such as control panels, remote controls, and handheld devices.

Brushless DC Motor

A brushless DC motor is a highly efficient electric motor that uses electronic commutation instead of brushes. It is used in drones, electric vehicles, and precision machinery due to its high speed, low noise, and long lifespan.

PIR Motion Sensor

A PIR (Passive Infrared) Motion Sensor detects motion by measuring changes in infrared radiation levels. It is commonly used in security systems, automatic lighting, and energy-saving devices to detect human movement.

28BYJ-48 Step Motor

The 28BYJ-48 Step Motor is a small, inexpensive stepper motor known for its high precision and ease of control. It is commonly used in robotics, automation projects, and position control applications.

Step Motor

A Step Motor moves in fixed angular steps, providing precise control over rotation and position. It is used in 3D printers, CNC machines, and camera platforms for accurate motion control.

Sonar Rangefinder

A Sonar Rangefinder measures distance by emitting sound waves and detecting the returning echoes. It is used in robotics, obstacle detection, and navigation systems to measure distances and avoid collisions.

Panel Mount 10K Potentiometer

A Panel Mount 10K Potentiometer is a variable resistor that adjusts voltage and current in a circuit. It is commonly used for controlling volume, brightness, and other adjustable settings in electronic devices.

Panel Mount Push Button Switch

A Panel Mount Push Button Switch is a momentary switch that completes a circuit when pressed. It is widely used for user input, control panels, and reset functions in electronic projects.

Rotary Encoder

A Rotary Encoder converts rotational motion into electrical signals. It is used to measure position, speed, and direction, commonly found in digital volume controls, navigation systems, and industrial automation.
